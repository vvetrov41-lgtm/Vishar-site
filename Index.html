Initial Care (Days 1-3)

  • ✓ Wash hands and remove dressing.
  • ✓ Quick lukewarm shower with mild soap.
  • ✓ Pat dry with a clean paper towel.
  • ✓ Apply a very thin layer of moisturiser.
Routine: Repeat wash, dry, and cream 3-5 times per day.

Film Application

  • ✓ Wear for 3-5 days.
  • ✓ Showers are fine; avoid hot steam.
  • ✓ Remove only when dry. Lift edge and stretch slowly.

FAQ

Common Questions

A standard session is up to 7 hours. Approximate estimates: Full sleeve: 8-10 sessions. Shoulder: 2-3 sessions. Forearm: 1-2 sessions. Colour work usually takes longer than black & grey.
I don't recommend numbing creams by default: risk of allergy, skin reacts unpredictably, healing can be less stable. Cream is applied only to broken skin towards the end of long sessions if necessary.
Surface heals in 10-14 days. Full healing: 4-6 weeks. Avoid heavy sweating/gym for 7-10 days. No sauna, pool, sea, or tanning for at least 3-4 weeks.
