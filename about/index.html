Vladimir Vishar.

Realism tattoo artist · Manchester & Salford

I'm Vladimir Vishar, a Manchester-based tattoo artist specialising in realistic tattooing. My practice focuses on two primary disciplines: vivid Colour Realism and evocative Black & Grey work.

I also specialise in cover-up tattoos — transforming old or unwanted ink into pieces my clients are genuinely proud of.

Every design I create is a unique collaboration — built from scratch around the client's concept, their body, and their skin. I don't use templates or repeat designs. Each piece is one of a kind.

I work with a high-end medical-grade finish and take hygiene and safety extremely seriously. If you have allergies, medical conditions, or any concerns, I'll work with you to make sure everything is safe and comfortable.

The Studio

No Regrets Studios Manchester

I work from No Regrets Studios in Salford — a professional, clean, and welcoming environment with everything needed for long sessions.

The studio is equipped with dedicated workstations, medical-grade sterilisation, and a comfortable setup designed for sessions of up to 7 hours.
