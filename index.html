FAQ

Do you do cover-ups?

Yes. Please keep in mind that dark tattoos can only be covered with darker designs, and usually the new tattoo needs to be larger. Send me a clear photo of the old tattoo and a full photo of the area where it is placed.

Pricing / minimum charge

Pricing depends on size, placement, style, and detail level. I’ll confirm an estimate after you send references and we do a quick consultation.

Booking & deposit

Booking is done via the form link. A deposit is required to secure your date. Details are shared during booking.

Turnaround time

It depends on the idea and current schedule. Smaller projects can be done sooner, bigger ones may need planning.

Healing basics

Keep it clean, avoid rubbing, moisturise lightly, and don’t pick. Full instructions are in the PDF.
