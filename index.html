FAQ

Do you do cover‑ups?

Yes. Please keep in mind that dark tattoos can only be covered with darker designs, and usually the new tattoo needs to be larger. Send me a clear photo of the old tattoo and a full photo of the area where it is placed.

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing depends on the size and complexity of the design. There is a minimum charge for small pieces to cover setup and material costs. Larger pieces are quoted individually during consultation.

How does booking and deposit work?

To secure your session a deposit is required. This deposit goes toward your total and is non‑refundable. Dates can be rescheduled with sufficient notice. Contact me via email or Instagram to arrange a booking.

How long is the turnaround time?

I typically book a few months in advance. After your initial consultation we'll agree on a date that works for both of us.

What are the basics of healing?

Keep the tattoo clean, avoid scratching or picking the area and apply a thin layer of recommended ointment. Full details are in the downloadable aftercare PDF.
