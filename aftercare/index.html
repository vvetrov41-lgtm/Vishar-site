Home/Aftercare

Guide

Heal it properly.

The first 14 days define the life of your tattoo.

🧼

Essentials

🖐️Always wash your hands before touching the tattoo.
🛏️Fresh bed sheets for the first night.
👕Wear loose clothing that won't rub.
⏱️

Initial Care

Day 0 (Evening)

If your session ended in the evening, keep the initial dressing on until morning.

Day 1

✓Wash hands and remove the dressing.
✓Quick lukewarm shower with mild fragrance-free soap.
✓Pat dry with a clean paper towel.
✓Apply a very thin layer of moisturiser.
🔁

Routine (Days 1–3)

Repeat 3–5 times per day

1
Gentle but thorough wash
2
Pat dry
3
Thin layer of cream + clean dressing

Disposable absorbent pad (puppy pad):

Breathable; use for sleep, travel, or under clothing. Change when damp.

🌙

Late Stage (Day 4+)

✓Once weeping stops and peeling starts, stop using dressings.
✓Light moisturising as needed.
✕Don't pick at the peeling skin.
🩹

Application

✓Wear for 3–5 days (up to 7 if comfortable and edges aren't lifting).
✓Showers are fine; avoid long hot steam.

When to remove early:

If fluid builds up, it burns/leaks, or edges lift — switch to regular care routine.

🧻

Removal Process

✓Removal: only when dry.
✓Lift an edge and stretch it parallel to the skin, slowly.

After removal:

WASH→PAT DRY→CREAM
💧

Moisturiser

Bepanthen Tattoo

Intense Care Ointment with Provitamin B5.

Contains lanolin; use alternative if sensitive.
Shop ↗
🚫

Avoid

💧Baths, pools, saunas
☀️Sun/Sunbeds (3–4 weeks)
✕Friction & tight synthetics
🐾Animal fur & dirty surfaces
✕Shaving & self-tan
🍷Alcohol (first 48h)
🏋️Intense workouts (3–7 days)
☀️ After healing, always use SPF 50 on the tattoo.
✅

Normal Symptoms

Mild redness, swelling, itch and peeling for 2–7 days.

⚠️

Contact me / See a doctor if

Pain or redness worsens after 48 hours.

Pus, bad odour, fever, or red streaks appear.

Severe rash or blisters (possible allergy).
